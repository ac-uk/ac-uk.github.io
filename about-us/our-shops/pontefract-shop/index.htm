    THE ALL NEW

    Airedale Computers Pontefract

    29 Market Place Pontefract WF8 1AG   Tel. 01977 329442

    Our new supersized retail and repair shop and Gamezone stands in the main market place in Pontefract town centre, opposite Bodycare and next to Bennets Butchers.

    We have a 5 meter long wall just for Keyboards with plenty of choice for business users and gamers alike.

    Our opposite wall is filled with all the best headsets, earplugs and mice with plenty of choice for de=iscerning gamers.

    We stock a large range of refurbished computers from just £99 along with our refurbished laptops from £50. We also have brand new laptops and computers. Usually ready built, we have a DUALCORE (2 cores), a QUADCORE (4 cores) and an OctoCore (8 cores) plus several fantastic New or Refurbished GAMERS

    Our range of refurbished gamer PC's start from just £299 including gamer keyboard & mouse, TFT screen and is currently fitted with a decent graphics card for gaming. Plenty of upgrade options or we can custom build for you.

    .....and then there is the GAMEZONE (almost finished) for gaming and tournaments, open to age 12 upwards.

    Computer and Laptop Repairs  are part of everyday life for our technicians in the shops. They are well qualified and have many years of experience. Our minimum bench fee for diagnostics is just £10 but that is deducted from any repairs. Turnaround is usually next day, often repairs are completed the same day depending on the queue and ordering of parts.
